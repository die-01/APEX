Our Story

We are a construction company based in Bujumbura, specializing in building construction, renovation, structural work, and landscaping. We believe in quality workmanship, respecting standards, deadlines, and budgets. Our commitment is the complete satisfaction of our clients.

SERVICES

We offer a wide range of services in the field of construction, tailored to the needs of individuals, businesses and institutions.
Namely:

Preconstruction Planning

Pre-construction planning is essential for efficient building projects. We optimize resource coordination, establish realistic construction budgets, and proactively identify potential construction challenges. This minimizes risks, streamlines workflows, and ensures on-time, within-budget delivery of your new construction or renovation project.

Architectural Modelling

Our architectural modeling services utilize cutting-edge digital tools to create precise visual and functional representations of your building design. We help clients visualize construction projects, make informed decisions, and optimize outcomes before breaking ground. Benefit from 3D modeling and architectural visualization for a clear project preview.

Construction Management

Comprehensive construction management ensures every phase of your building project is expertly overseen. We guarantee adherence to schedules and stringent quality standards through efficient coordination, meticulous budget control, and unwavering commitment to construction safety.

Building construction

We construct residential, commercial, and institutional buildings, from foundation to finishing. Our team is committed to delivering solid, durable structures that meet all applicable standards.

Structural work

Foundations, load-bearing walls, columns, beams, slabs, floors... we master all aspects of structural work with precision and professionalism.

Renovation and rehabilitation

Whether it's to modernize a building or restore it, we intervene carefully to respect the existing structure while providing comfort and aesthetics.
